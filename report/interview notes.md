# Pros of Vocab Express
The leader-boards on Vocab Express encouraged a lot of competition.
Vocab Express allowed the teachers to see how much time students were putting
in.
However competition isn't useful for students who aren't near the top of the
leader-board.

# Cons of Vocab Express
Vocab Express enforced that each word had one correct translation which was very
annoying.
Vocab Express was strict about spelling mistakes even in English which was
problematic for kids with dyslexia.
It would be good if you could set different spelling rules between the groups to
be more lenient on students with dyslexia.
(This didn't come up in our conversation, but for assignments the groups
probably need to be mutually exclusive, and span the whole class. So this
dyslexia thing would have to be a separate system).

With Vocab Express many students would screenshot the words to cheat, she
doesn't think this is a big problem.

Be very lenient with typos.
